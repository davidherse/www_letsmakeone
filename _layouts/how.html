How we work

  1. Raise Investment: We collect thousands of small investments from people like you using equity crowd funding – which is a way for business to sell shares to the public.

  2. Research: We form an expert team to research ideas, products, innovations, markets. We figure out the best way to make the biggest impact profitably.

  3. Vote: We present business plans for the most promising solutions to you our investors and you get to vote on your preferred direction.

  4. Test: We start a pilot project and test the chosen idea is viable, feasible, desirable and sustainable.

  5. Scale: If the pilot is successful, we create a subsidiary business and scale it.

  6. Pay: When the business is making sustainable profits, we pass them back to you.

  7. Repeat: Then, we do it again.
